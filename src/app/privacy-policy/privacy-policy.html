Processing Activities

Collection of General Information When Visiting the Website

Type and Purpose of Processing

When you access the website, general information is automatically collected. This information (server log files) includes the type of web browser, the operating system used, the domain name of the internet service provider, the IP address, and similar data.

It is processed in particular for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring a smooth connection to the website
  • Ensuring smooth use of the website
  • Ensuring and evaluating system security and stability, in particular for abuse detection
  • Technically error-free presentation and optimization of the website

The data is not used to draw conclusions about individual persons. However, we reserve the right to subsequently review server log files should concrete evidence of unlawful use arise.

Legal Basis and Legitimate Interest

Processing is carried out pursuant to Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR on the basis of our legitimate interest in improving the stability and functionality of the website and ensuring system security and abuse detection.

Recipients

Recipients of the data may include technical service providers acting as processors for the operation and maintenance of the website.

Storage Period

Data is stored in server log files for a maximum of 7 days, unless a security-relevant event occurs (e.g. a DDoS attack). In such a case, server log files are stored until the security-relevant event has been resolved and fully investigated.

Provision Required or Necessary

The provision of the aforementioned personal data is neither legally nor contractually required. However, without the IP address, the service and functionality of the website cannot be guaranteed. In addition, individual services and features may be unavailable or restricted.

Contact

Type and Purpose of Processing

Contact may be made via the provided email addresses. In this case, the personal data transmitted with the email will be stored. This includes the date and time of sending, the email address, IP addresses, and information about the servers involved in the email communication.

Contact is also possible via the provided phone numbers. In this case, log data is collected that includes the phone number and the duration of the call.

Regardless of the communication channel chosen, the content of the request is collected. The data is stored for the purpose of individual communication.

Legal Basis

Data is processed on the basis of a legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). The legitimate interest lies in enabling straightforward contact.

If contact is made to request a quote, the data is processed for the purpose of carrying out pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Recipients

Recipients of the data may include technical service providers acting as processors for the operation and maintenance of the website.

Storage Period

Data is deleted upon full completion of the correspondence, provided no statutory retention obligations apply.

If a contractual relationship arises, the statutory retention periods of generally 6 or 10 years apply for the purpose of proper accounting and tax requirements.

Provision Required or Necessary
